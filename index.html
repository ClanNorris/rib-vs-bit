Time your tongue attack to catch flies or hit your opponent before they hit you. No account or setup needed — just open the page and play.

About

Rib vs Bit is a free browser-based multiplayer arcade game. Two players control frogs and compete to catch flies with their tongues. The first frog to land 3 captures wins the match.

There's nothing to download or install — the game runs instantly in your web browser. Matches are fast, usually just a few minutes, making Rib vs Bit easy to jump into for a quick round or a rematch.

The game is built with Phaser and uses real-time multiplayer, so both players see the same match unfold live. Rib vs Bit is an independent project developed under Clan Norris, with a "Super Turbo" follow-up edition currently in development.
